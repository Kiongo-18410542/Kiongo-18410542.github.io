Anime is an abbreviation of the word animation. In Japan the word refers to all forms of animation. However, outside japan, it become a word to describe animation from Japan.

The reason I love anime is because it’s different to most American animation like “Batman” and “Spider-man”. The difference can be seen in the artwork of storytelling, breadth of material and even cultural nuances exhibited by the characters.
One of the longest anime running is One Piece which is still on air for 20 years! It is one of my favourite because of the world, characters and emotional connection with the viewers.
